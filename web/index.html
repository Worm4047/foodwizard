slow cooker chai

The cheapest and the easiesy way is to cook with what you already have.

- Raj Kumar(Food Wizard)

Steps

  • Pour water into the crock of a slow cooker. Stir in the ginger, cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon sticks, and peppercorns. Turn to High; simmer for 8 hours.

  • Steep tea bags in the hot spiced water for 5 minutes. Strain tea into a clean container. Stir in sweetened condensed milk; serve hot.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 quarts water...15 slices fresh ginger, peeled...15 green cardamom pods, split open and seeded...25 whole cloves...3 cinnamon sticks...3 whole black peppercorns...8 black tea bags...1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
